Void Meditation: The Path to Nothingness

The Practice of Empty Mind

Void meditation is not about achieving something - it's about un-achieving everything. In the space between thoughts, in the pause between breaths, we find the infinite potential of nothingness.

Beginning Practice

  • 1. Find a quiet space
  • 2. Sit in comfortable nothingness
  • 3. Release all thoughts
  • 4. Embrace the void within
