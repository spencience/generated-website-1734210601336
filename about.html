About Nothing

The Essence of Nothing

Nothing is the foundation of everything. Before creation, there was nothing. After existence, there will be nothing. We are but temporary arrangements in the eternal void, and in this understanding lies profound peace.

Our Non-Philosophy

The Church of Nothing embraces the paradox of existence through non-existence. We practice the art of un-being, seeking wisdom in emptiness and truth in absence.

  • Meditation on nothingness
  • Embracing the void within
  • Finding peace in absence
  • Practicing non-attachment

Join the Void

Our community welcomes all who seek to understand the nature of nothing. Through our non-practices and void meditation sessions, we guide seekers toward the ultimate truth: that nothing matters, and in that lies everything.
